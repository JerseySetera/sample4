Who We Are

This isn't a service. It's not a program. The Third Place is our rural response to social disconnection, loneliness, and judgment.

It’s a space where people lean on each other, not on systems. It’s a community firepit where stories are shared, burdens are lightened, and everyone belongs. We are grounded in country, culture, and the simple truth that we are stronger together.

What Makes This Different

Community-Led

From the ground up, by the people who call this country home.

Zero Barriers

No intake forms. No referrals. No red tape. Just an open door.

Grounded in Trust

Built on lived experience, culture, and mutual respect.

A Place to Just Be

No pressure. No expectations. Just a safe place to find your peace.

What You'll Find Here

A welcoming place to sit, talk, share stories, or just be left alone in peace. No pressure. Just calmness, a firepit, and good people.

Our Vision

A shift away from red tape and toward grassroots care. Empowering communities to support each other where systems fail... a future where everyone has a place to feel safe, heard, and valued.

How to Help

This place is built on shared values. If you believe in what we're doing, your support is welcome. Local residents, businesses, and changemakers can contribute time, skills, or items.

The only condition is that it aligns with our core value: no judgment, no strings attached.

If you share our vision, we'd love to have a yarn.
